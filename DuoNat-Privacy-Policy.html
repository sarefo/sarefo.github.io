Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-02-01

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DuoNat ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our web application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app (e.g., clicks, page views).
  • iNaturalist Data: Our app retrieves publicly available data from iNaturalist, which we store in a database for caching.
  • Local Storage Data: Some data may be stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB (Dexie) for performance and offline functionality.
  • Personal Data (if applicable): If users create an account, we collect names, email addresses, and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the app's functionality.
  • To personalize your experience (e.g., saving quiz progress).
  • To analyze app performance and usage trends.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • We do not sell your data.
  • Data retrieved from iNaturalist remains subject to iNaturalist's privacy policies.
  • We may use analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve our service.

5. Your Rights

  • You can clear your local storage data at any time from your browser settings.
  • You may request access or deletion of your data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Any changes will be posted here.
