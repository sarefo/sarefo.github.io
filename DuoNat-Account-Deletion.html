Account Deletion Instructions

To delete your DuoNat account and all associated data, please follow these steps in the app:

1 Tap the menu button on the top right
2 Tap on "Sign in" if not already signed in
3 Tap on Settings
4 Choose the "Profile" tab
5 Click on "Delete my account data"
6 Follow the instructions on screen to complete the process

Note: This action cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
